About the Program

Designed for future-ready marketers who want to play on the global stage while staying grounded in local realities.

You’ll build a complete end-to-end content marketing system tailored to emerging markets like Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Sub-Saharan Africa. From strategy to SEO-driven production, multi-platform distribution, and performance analytics—everything is powered by AI.

The final project is a real, portfolio-ready campaign you can pitch: audience research, UVP, content calendar, assets, distribution plan, and a dashboard for insights.

What you’ll master

Practical skills with modern tools. No fluff.

Strategy

Data-driven audience insights, emerging market ecosystems, UVP crafting, content roadmaps.

Production

SEO writing, visuals, short-form videos—accelerated with ChatGPT, Gemini, and Canva Magic Media.

Distribution

Platform-native playbooks, calendar orchestration, paid/organic balance, localization.

Analytics

KPI trees, dashboards, attribution basics, iteration with AI-assisted insights.
