Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the Lensetek AI Mate website and mobile application (the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and identity data: name, email address, profile photo (if you choose to link/sign in with Google or another provider).
  • Content you submit: text, images, prompts, and conversation context you provide for our AI features to process.
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser, device/installation identifiers, error logs, and diagnostics to maintain performance and security.
  • Usage data: feature interactions, access times, preferences you choose, and aggregate metrics to improve the product.
  • Optional device permissions (only if you enable them): camera/microphone for input, local storage for saving/exporting files, notifications, and approximate location when a feature requires it.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: essential cookies for authentication and security; analytics cookies/SDKs to understand aggregate usage.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and operate the Service, including processing AI requests and syncing the web and app experience.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, spam, or harmful activity.
  • Deliver customer support and service-related updates.
  • Analyze and improve product performance (using aggregated and de-identified data where possible).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms of use.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Cloud and security providers for hosting, backups, and attack mitigation.
  • Authentication providers (e.g., Google) if you choose that sign-in method.
  • Analytics/performance providers for aggregate statistics and performance monitoring.
  • AI providers that process your inputs to generate responses (e.g., Google Gemini). We do not use your data to train our own models; data sent to providers is subject to their policies.
  • Law enforcement or others when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and compliance.

4. Storage and Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes described or as required by law. Technical logs are retained for limited periods based on operational and security needs.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures such as encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and monitoring to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; some residual risk may remain.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Request access to, correction, update, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Decline or withdraw consent for specific permission-based processing (e.g., camera, microphone, location, notifications).
  • Disable non-essential cookies via browser/device settings.
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications via the provided links or by contacting us.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children under those requirements.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to ensure equivalent protection in line with applicable laws.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for security and login. Analytics cookies/SDKs are used only for aggregate statistics. You can manage cookies via browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect basic functions.

10. Device Permissions (Web & Mobile)

  • Camera/Microphone: only when you choose to record or upload content.
  • Storage/Photos: to save outputs or pick files you want to send.
  • Notifications: for alerts or updates you opt into.
  • Approximate location: only if a feature requires it and after you grant permission.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app, website, or email. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent change.

12. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact us at support@lensetek.com.
